# 🛍️ Retail Sales Analytics using SQL
Advanced SQL portfolio project analyzing retail sales, customer behavior, and business KPIs using Joins, CTEs, Window Functions, and Subqueries.


## 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates advanced SQL skills by analyzing a retail sales database to answer real-world business questions and generate actionable insights.

The project includes data modeling, complex SQL queries, KPI reporting, and business analysis using MySQL.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

To analyze customer purchasing behavior, product performance, supplier contribution, and sales trends for better business decision-making.

---

## 🗂️ Database Tables

- Customers
- Orders
- Order_Items
- Products
- Suppliers
- Shippers
- Payment_Methods

---

## 📊 Business KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Total Units Sold
- Average Product Price
- Average Revenue per Customer
- Best Selling Product
- Highest Revenue Category
- Average Orders per Customer

## ❓ Business Questions Solved

- Revenue generated by each shipper
- Highest spending customers
- Product category performance
- Customer purchase behavior
- Product ranking
- Monthly revenue growth
- Customer loyalty segmentation
- Supplier performance
- Revenue contribution by city
- Business KPI dashboard
